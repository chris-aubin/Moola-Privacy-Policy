Log Data

When you use Moola, in the case of an error in the app, data and information (through third party products) is collected from your phone in the form log data. This data is used to aid in the diagnosing and addressing of the error in question. This log data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app at the time that the error was encountered, the time and date at which the error was encountered, and other statistics.

Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory.

Moola does not use these cookies explicitly. However, the app may use third party code and libraries that use cookies to collect information and improve their services. You may either permit or refuse the use of these cookies. If you choose to refuse the use of cookies, you may not be able to use some aspects of Moola.
